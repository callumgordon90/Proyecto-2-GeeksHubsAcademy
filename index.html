The English Restaurant

For over 150 years we have been the only restaurant in the world to specialise exclusively in English Cuisine.

Welcome to the English Restaurant. Located in the hard of the old city in an exquisite Georgian building. We pride ourselves on our long family tradition as masters of haut cuisine which has been passed down from generation to generation for over 150 years. An authentic taste of the British Isles and a dining experience which cannot be matched anywhere else. Come and enjoy yourself amidst the most elegant and luxurious surroundings and indulge yourself in the sensual comestibles of this fine island!
